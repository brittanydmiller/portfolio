Look Ahead to February 24th.   

You've just turned in your Z-Fest Film!  

What to do now?
Come to the FREE 2013 Academy Awards Party 
at Theatres at Mall of America®! 

After you've spent all your money creating your Z-Fest film, it will be the perfect time for a free event! The Theatres at Mall of America invites the Z-Fest filmmakers to join them for their 2013 Academy Awards Party! After you turn in your Z-Fest project on February 24, you can sit back in the theatre's oversized seats and comment on snubbed nominees with your friends.

 

The Theatres applauds your efforts in keeping our film industry alive and thriving. As a thank you, we invite you to join Tim Kelly of Chud.com, for an unforgettable free event on Sunday, Feb. 24 at 6:00 p.m.

You'll enjoy:
  • Drink specials and free appetizers in the STARBAR starting at 6:00pm.
  • VIP (21+) Seating
  • Live broadcast of the 85th Annual Academy Awards.  
  • Commentary by Tim Kelly, games and prizes throughout the show.  
  • After the show, don't forget to pick-up your VIP Swag bag.

This event is FREE and open to all members of the Minnesota film community along with their family and friends. To RSVP, please send the names of each person in your party to Emerald Swearingen, Theatres at Mall of America Special Event Coordinator, at emerald.swearingen@moa.net. Space is limited, so RSVP as soon as possible. This is a 21+ event, so please bring a valid ID/Driver's license.

 

For updates, events and special programing, follow the Theatres on Facebook and Twitter.
