New Sony 4K Cameras Coming Exclusively to Z Systems this February


Sony extends the options for shooters with the new F5 and F55 digital cinema cameras -- smaller, high-performing 4K cameras that offer a great price and superb performance.  Both of these full-featured cameras were designed to fill the gap between the workhorse F3 and the high end F65.   

 

Z Systems is your exclusive, local reseller for these Sony CineAlta 4K products. Stay tuned for upcoming seminars and events focused on the cameras, their workflows and all their new accessories -- including the new AXS-R5 RAW onboard recorder. 

 

Here's a quick comparison of their differences:

  • Check out the PMW-F55 if you want to record 4K, 2K, or HD internally to SxS Pro+ cards, want electronic frame image scan, and want the same type of color filter array technology used in Sony's F65. 
  • Check out the PMW-F5 if you have a smaller budget, don't need electronic frame image scan, and don't need to record 4K internally (you can still record 2K or HD internally to SxS Pro+ cards). It has an F3-type color filter array. 

 

Both of these cameras can accept the AXS-R5 RAW onboard recorder.

 

Electronic frame image scan eliminates 'jello effect' on objects moving across the frame and distortion causing vertical lines to tilt.

 

No matter what your budget is, Sony and Z Systems have a solution for you! Learn more about Sony's entire line of large-format cameras on our website.
