It's ON, for the fourth year in a row.

Z-Fest is quickly turning in to one of the premier local film festivals.  So far, about 40 teams have signed up for this year's fest and will be competing for over $5,000 in prizes. We hope you can participate in the festival - either by joining, supporting, or coming out to the screenings to cheer on our incredibility talented local filmmakers. 

 

Here are some important dates to keep in mind:

  • February 1st - Team Registration Deadline (5pm) 
  • February 5th - Scripts Due
  • February 24th - Films and Trailers Due
  • February 27th - March 6th - Online Trailer Contest - be sure to log on to Facebook and vote for your favorite film! Cash prizes for the winners! 
  • March 6th and 7th - Screenings at the Riverview Theater and after parties (open to everyone, night-of ticketing only - first-come, first-served).
  • March 14th - 'Best of Fest' Theater Screening and Awards Ceremony at the Theatres Mall of America (advance tickets available soon on our website). 

 

Thanks to all the sponsors, organizers, judges, and participants - The last three years were a blast and this year surely won't disappoint! 

 

See you there!
